**Implementation B: HighPrecision Mode**

Enhanced FFT Pipeline for sub-bin frequency resolution:
1. Same windowing and FFT as SimpleFirst
2. Locate the highest magnitude peak in configurable vocal frequency range
3. Perform parabolic interpolation using the peak and its two nearest neighbors:
